Almond Coconut Lemon Cake

Many people have tried this now and no one thinks of it as a gluten free cake. Itâ€™s just a delicious cake! Love the coconut + lemon + almond meal combination, and the springy, moist crumb. BONUS: Just about the fastest, easiest cake recipe I know.

Ingredients list:

  • Almond meal (aka ground almonds)
  • Butter
  • Coconut
  • Sugar
  • Lemon
  • Eggs

Cooking Steps:

  1. Melt the butter
  2. Whisk in all the other wet ingredients
  3. Whisk in the almond meal, coconut, baking powder, salt and zest
  4. Bake 40 minutes
